About Us

“The only way to do great work is to love what you do” Student developer club is a technical society of our college aimed at providing and enhancing technical boom. We have volunteers for different fields like competitive coding, web development, App development, data science, graphic designing, who organize workshops, bootcamps and tech-fests on regular intervals for students to enjoy the environment they are aiming to work in.

“Coding is quickly becoming an essential career skill. Learning to code is a fantastic opportunity equalizer — if you’re good at it, it can help you achieve your dreams.”

When we compete it brings out our best capabilities. Our club also manages to create a competitive environment in college which is necessary in this fast paced world.
